About Mondial Media Workforce

Mondial Media Workforce a non-profit organisation working to improve the quality of online reporting. We started out in July 2009 and have since then grown to an organisation of 3 permanent staff-members and numerous volunteers. These staff-members are:


Kenneth Ferrer

Director at Debates4Groningen. Fred has a great social network and is present on every social evenement in Groningen. People love his enthusiasm for Groningen and its beauty. Besides being director at Debates4Groningen, Fred also is part-time photographer. In his movies, as well in his photos, you can see his keen eye for detail.


Jessica Lanois.

Jessica, or Jessie, is a presenter at TV MAX. Not a day is the same for her and that is what she loves about her job. This press event is the sole reason she is coming to Groningen. Jessie is a well-known person who attracts publicity and that is great news for this media conference!


Fred McMillan

In Kenneth's every day life he is an artist who is very active on social media. His twitter account has more than 85.000 followers. In his tweets he discusses art pieces and reviews media events and expositions. You should always be careful what you tell mister Ferrer. Before you know it, he'll send a tweet about what you said. So make sure you stay on his positive side.
