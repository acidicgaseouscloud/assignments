सत्यमेव जयते

"सत्यमेव जयते" or "Truth Alone Triumphs" is the most important tenet of good journalism. The mainstream press, long heralded as the fourth estate, has a duty to society. A duty to bring truth to the forefront and, in doing so, hold those in power accountable. In recent years, the journalism industry has been criticised for putting economic goals above the pursuit of truth. This, along with the increased accessibility to information, has opened up a new space for journalism.

In the digital age, there has been an exponential growth of the fifth estate. Bloggers and Journalists undertaking independent journalistic projects to discuss issues that don't make it in the mainstream press. What does this mean for the pursuit of truth, for journalism, for democracy?

The question, then, becomes "Has the digital age made it easier to voice truth?". This is the question we hope to examine through "Truth To Power". Join us in conversation with experts from diverse backgrounds and fields, as we discuss the implications of the digital age for journalism.
