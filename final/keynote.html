Asha Kulshreshtha

Asha Kulshreshtha is one of the Directors of "Stree", a non-profit organisation focusing on women's health and safety. She discusses how digital platforms allow them to achieve their goals by facilitating better contact with the women they work with. This further allows them to bring their everyday struggles to the forefront.

Jorge Varela

Jorge Varela is researcher with the Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México (UNAM). He is a specialist in citizen journalism and discusses the various ways in which it is affecting democracy. His discussion touches upon how citizen journalism is allowing for greater recognition of miscarriages of justice and increased accountability for those in power.

Robin

Robin is a non-binary researcher and social science communicator. They primarily focus on social media platforms. Their speech recognises the various ways in which social media platforms fail to curb the spread of misinformation and some measures that can be put in place to deal with false news more effectively.

Victoria Davenport

Victoria Davenport is a journalist with R.E.D. inc.'s online magazine. She discusses how the viral characteristic of the internet is difficult to counteract even with rigorously fact-checked articles. She explains that misinformation spreads but the corrections or criticisms often do not have the same reach as the original post.
