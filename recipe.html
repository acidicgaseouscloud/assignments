Cranberry Ice Cream Dish

How do you make Cranberry Ice Cream?

Posted by iteration 34,65,72,637 of "We"

We have been devout consumers of ice cream but we have never made it ourselves. However, the recipe for cranberry ice cream has been passed onto us by the internet. We now pass this knowledge on to you.

Most recipes distinguish between cranberry ice cream and cranberry sauce. They assure us that they taste magnificent together. But I shall stick to the ice cream, better people than me can tell you about the puree.

The yield should be approximately the population of the Netherlands, for convenience.

To make Cranberry Ice Cream, you will need:
  • 34836930 cups of heavy cream
  • 34836930 cups of milk
  • 26127697.5 cups of sugar
  • 104510790 egg yolks
  • 2177308.125 teaspoons of salt
  • 69673860 cups of cranberries
  • 17,418,465 sticks of cinnamon
If you have scoured all the Albert Heijns for the ingredients, this is how you make the ice cream:
  1. Put the sugar and the heavy cream in an enormous custom made bowl, the size of Amsterdam. Boil it until the sugar has disolved and half the population of Amsterdam has a heat stroke.
  2. In another gigantic bowl, beat the egg yolks, salt, and Search Results 52255395 tablespoons of sugar until it has tripled in volume and engulfed Eindhoven.
  3. Mix the egg yolk mixture and the heavy cream mixture in a saucepan. Cook on low heat till it becomes slightly thick (77ยบ Celsius)
  4. Pour mixture through a sieve and let it cool to room temperature. Stir occassionally.
  5. Put the cranberries, remaining sugar, and cinnamon into a saucepan. Boil over slightly high heat. Keep stirring
  6. Bring to low heat and stir until the cranberries explode over most of north Netherlands. Blend till its smooth like Santana.
  7. Mix both mixtures, stir to remove lumps. Transfer to a container and freeze for about 4-6 hours.
There you have it folks! Cranberry Ice Cream!
